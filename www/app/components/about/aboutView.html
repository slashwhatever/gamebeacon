gamebeacon for Destiny is currently in Alpha!

This means you will find bugs. When you spot anything unusual or if just have some feedback about your experience with the app, please email us.

We are monitoring how the app is used but nothing beats good old fashioned user feedback. Just try keep it constructive :)

FAQ

  1. Q: Is the app free?

  2. A: Yes. It will remain free as long as we can afford the server costs :)

  3. Q: How can you afford to have a free app?

  4. A: We can't. There's a donate button at the bottom of this page and in the action menu. If you find the app useful and want to support the ongoing development costs, please consider donating a few beans.

  5. Q: Is there a benefit to donating?

  6. A: not right now. You just get that warm fuzzy feeling knowing you helped keep something alive for the greater good. We may build in donator-only features later, depending on feedback and we may ask donators if they'd like their name features in a special "Credits" page at a later date.

  7. Q: Are you going to sell my data to pay the bills?

  8. A: No. We hate spam as much as you. We will never give, sell or otherwise pass your data to anyone else. Ever.

  9. Q: So why do you need my email address?

  10. A: Email verification helps keep the app secure. It also means if we need to contact you about an issue with the app, we have a way of doing so.

  11. Q: Will there be a version for iOS?

  12. A: We hope so. Development costs time and money. We're working on it but we need your help to develop the app further.

  13. Q: Is the app finished?

  14. A: Hell no! We've got a ton of extra features on the road map and will look to enable them as soon as we can. You can also influence the future of the app by emailing us and suggesting features you'd like to see.
