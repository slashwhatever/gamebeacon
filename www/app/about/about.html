FAQ

  1. Q: Is the app free?

  2. A: Yes. It will remain free as long as we can afford the server costs :)

  3. Q: How can you afford to have a free app?

  4. A: Honestly, we can't. If you find the app useful and want to support the ongoing development, please rate us and give us constructive feedback via email.

  5. Q: Are you going to sell my data to pay the bills?

  6. A: No. We hate spam as much as you. We will never give, sell or otherwise pass your data to anyone else. Ever.

  7. Q: So why do you need my email address?

  8. A: Email verification helps keep the app secure. It also means if we need to contact you about an issue with the app, we have a way of doing so.

  9. Q: Is the app finished?

  10. A: No! We've got a ton of extra features on the road map and will look to enable them as soon as we can. You can also influence the future of the app by emailing us and suggesting features you'd like to see. We are monitoring how the app is used but nothing beats good old fashioned user feedback. Just try keep it constructive :)
